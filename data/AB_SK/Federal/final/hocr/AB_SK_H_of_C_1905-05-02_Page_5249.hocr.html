Sir, I desire to revert once more to that feature of the constitution, because to my mind it is an all important question. In discussing this matter in parliament hon. members may have different opinions, the opposition may differ from the government, hon. members on the same side of the House may differ from one another. I am safe in saying that constitutional mat- ters of this kind, matters of great weight and moment, do not end in parliament.
