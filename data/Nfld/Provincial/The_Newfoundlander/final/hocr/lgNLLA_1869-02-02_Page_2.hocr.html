REGULATIONS FOR LEN T.

We are requested to announce that the following (being last year's Lenten Regulations) will be also the Regulations for the ensuing Lent within the Diocess of St. John's:­

1. Every day except Sunday is a fast day on one meal and a collation. 2. Flesh meat is allowed at one meal on Sundays, Mondays, Tuesdays and Thursdays and on St. Patrick's day, except the first four drys of Lent and the last six days of Holy Week. But fish and flesh are not to be used at the same meal. 3. E.g4s (except Fridays) butter m k, cheese an I whitemeats, are allowed at one meal every day, except Ash Wednesd-y, Spy-Wednesday, and Good Friday. 4. The aged, the young under 21 years, those obliged to bard labor, the sick or weakly, nurses and womat, about to become mothers are exeuipt from the fast. In casus of doubt you can consult your Confessors who are enpowered to give the necessary dispensations. Sr. John's. Feb, 1, 1869.
