Every inland country requires a seaboard on which to receive and ship material, and it thus becomes a necessity for Upper Canada to unite with the Maritime Provinces. The French peoplc of Canada are industrious, kind and frugal, but they are not a pro- gressive people. In the House of As- sembly in Canada he heard the leading Lower Canadian statesmen oppose a measure providing for Light Houses.
