Their lordships are of opinion that there is not the least colour for such a contention. The words of the statute are apt to authorize the utmost discretion of enactments for the at- tainment of the objects pointed to. They are words under which the widest departure from criminal procedure as it is known and prac- tised in this country have been authorized in Her Majesty’s Indian empire. Forms of pro- cedure unknown to the English common law have there been established and acted upon, and to throw the least doubt upon the validity of powers conveyed by those words would be of widely mischievous consequence.
