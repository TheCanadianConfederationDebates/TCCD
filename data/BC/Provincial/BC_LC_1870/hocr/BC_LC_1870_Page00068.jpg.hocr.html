He said :—It is pretty generally known that a company was started for the construction of a Grnving Dock at Esquimalt, and that negotiations with that object in view had taken place under a former administration; but although both the Admirals and Governor Seymour had reported in favour of it, the thing had fallen through. owing to the inability of the company to obtain the necessary funds.
