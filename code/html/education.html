Mini-Units

Mini modules

We have developed free mini-units for grade 7/8 and high school classes. Each mini unit caters to provincial histories and curriculum requirements.

Nous avons mis au point des plans de leçons gratuits pour les classes de 7e et de 8e années, ainsi que pour les écoles secondaires. Chaque plan met l'accent sur l'histoire des provinces concernées et respecte les exigences pédagogiques de ces dernières.

Several of the mini-units are available in French.

Plusieurs de ces mini modules sont disponibles en anglais.

Provincial Intermediate / Senior Mini Units

Mini modules provinciale pour les niveaux intermédiaire et supérieur

The provincial intermediate / senior mini units focus on a specific province's journey into Confederation. Each mini-unit conforms to provincial curriculum requirements and allows students to engage with the biographies and statements of provincial, federal, and Indigenous historical figures who adopted pro- and anti-Confederation positions.

National Senior Mini-Units

Mini modules pour les écoles secondaires

The national senior mini units focus the country’s Confederation debates. Each mini-unit conforms to provincial curriculum requirements and contains group activities that allow students to engage with the biographies and statements of provincial, federal historical figures from every province who adopted pro- and anti-Confederation positions. They also contain lesson plans that encourage cultural understanding and reconciliation between Indigenous and non-Indigenous Peoples.
